HOUSE RULES


Let's make the most out of this stay and be respectful with others.


In the house

Please TAKE YOUR SHOES OFF at the bottom of the stairs especially during inclement weather. The upstairs is almost 100% carpet.

  • No guests above the number provided in the booking are permitted on the property, in the unit or in pool area at any time. No more than a maximum of 8 guests can stay in the upper unit at any one time.
  • No smoking inside. When outside make sure all doors and windows are closed to not permit smoke from coming in to the house.
  • Please do not bring your pets. As much as we love them, they are not allowed in the house.

Party policy

Parties are absolutely NOT ALLOWED

This property is smack in the midst of a residential neighborhood. A party is defined as any activity which is likely to annoy the neighbors- rude behavior, loud music, yelling to name a few. This policy gives us the right, at our discretion to evict guests without refund in response to neighbor complaints.

We call it the Be Cool Rule. If what you're doing doesn't harm the property or annoy the neighbors, it's cool. We are very serious about this rule. We cannot have disruptive guests in our property; it has a huge effect on the vacation rental industry in Austin and gives us all a very bad reputation. Please respect this property, and our neighbors.


Pool & hot tub

Please respect our neighbors, keep all music, noise & voice level to a low / minimum in the pool and balcony. Absolutely no yelling, boom boxes or loud music.

  • Access to the pool is from the path to you left of the front door. The stairs and access from the garage area are off limit to the tenants.
  • Hot tub should be requested/reserved at booking time. The hot tub is optional and should be requested in your booking. Please contact us if you have questions.
  • Children must always be supervised.
  • Wear shoes when stepping on the deck - it gets very hot.
  • No running on pool deck.

  • NO DIVING! The pool is shallow.
  • Pool hours are from 8am to 11pm strictly enforced in respect to our neighbors and their young babies and children.
  • NO GLASS of any kind in the pool area (bottles, jugs, pitchers etc.).
  • The number of guests in the dwelling as well as number of guests in the pool area are restricted to the number of guests registered in the booking.

  • The toilet facility is located under the deck for your use.
  • Pool items are there for your use most are in the brown storage box, please return them when you are done using them.
  • Do not leave any items floating in the pool when you are done. We would also ask that all the tenants please make sure you take back upstairs everything you bring to the pool area.

Hope the weather is good, and you are able to enjoy the pool!


Car parking

Please do not block the two-car garage door on the right side.

For parking, use the driveway, park in front of the one-car garage door along the black fence. The three available parking spots are illustrated in the picture below.
